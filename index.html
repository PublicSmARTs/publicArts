We love public art, and we know we're not alone. This webpage aims compile individuals' knowledge about local art to create a resource for finding public anywhere in the world. Anyone can add, update, and discuss a work of public art, ultimately creating a robust, crowd-sourced set of data. Whether you are traveling to a new place or exploring your own city, the webpage will help you see the beauty around you. For free.
